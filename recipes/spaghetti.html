Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

This spaghetti and meatballs recipe can be found on Youtube by Joshua Weissman, alongside more detailed steps and ingredients. This recipe required you to make your own meatballs which were very tasty. The inclusion of the basil and parmesan toppings really elevated the dish and is one I will most likely make again. I did not, however, make my own spaghetti.

Ingredients

Meatballs:

  • 1/4 lb ham
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp finely ground fennel seed
  • 1 and 3/4ths tsp fine sea salt
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 crushed panko bread crumbs
  • 1 eggs

Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of basil
  • Freshly grated parmesan for topping
  • Chiffonade basil for topping

Steps

  1. Combine all meatball ingredients except for the panko and egg. Mix well.
  2. Add panko and egg, mix till emulsified.
  3. Use scoop to shape them into balls, onto a baking sheet.
  4. Add olive oil to pan, sear meatballs. You don't have to cook them fully.
  5. Start making the sauce! Add garlic in the same pan and sautee.
  6. Add pepper flakes, saute for 30s. Add tomatoes with some salt and a pinch of sugar.
  7. Add meatballs. Bring to a simmer and reduce heat to medium-low, simmering for 5-8 minutes. Add basil halfway through. Make sure meatballs are cooked all the way.
  8. Assemble and serve!
